About

  • November 2016

    Our Humble Beginnings

    One Foot In kicked off of a fateful conversation at the Tufts Venture Lab. Since then we have talked to high school students in the United States of America and abroad (Bangladesh, Indonesia, Germany, India, and China to name a few countries), and realized that it wasn’t just the three of us who had been stressed during application season!

  • December 2016

    An Agency is Born

    We offered our service to students in the USA and abroad pro bono first, so that we could thoroughly understand the needs of our clients before we started making sales. We continue to listen to your experience using our service, so that we our reviewers can continuously strive to serve you better!

  • February 2017

    Transition to Full Service

    We boast of a team of reviewers from selective colleges like Harvard, Tufts, University of Rochester, and more. Our reviewers are prime communicators in their schools and are eager to listen and help. We are continuously growing our networks and continue to add more value to our service.
